Overview

Istanbul, the vibrant and historic city straddling the continents of Europe and Asia, offers an enchanting blend of cultures, sights, and experiences that captivate every traveler’s heart. As Turkey’s cultural and economic hub, Istanbul seamlessly fuses its rich heritage with modernity, creating an unforgettable journey for visitors.

The city is home to some of the world’s most iconic landmarks, including the awe-inspiring Hagia Sophia, the majestic Blue Mosque, and the grand Topkapi Palace, each bearing witness to Istanbul’s illustrious past. Wandering through the bustling streets, you’ll find an array of delightful bazaars, where you can haggle for unique souvenirs, immerse yourself in the aromatic spices, and savor traditional Turkish delights.

Istanbul 6-Day Tour Plan

Day 1: Arrival & Istanbul City Orientation

Activities & Sightseeing:

  • Arrival at Istanbul Airport, transfer to hotel
  • Evening city orientation tour: Sultanahmet Square, Hippodrome
  • Leisure time to relax or enjoy hotel facilities

Facilities Included:

  • Airport pick-up and drop
  • Hotel check-in and welcome drinks
  • English-speaking tour guide

Meals:

  • Welcome dinner at local Turkish restaurant (kebabs, mezes, dessert)
Day 2: Iconic Istanbul Landmarks

Activities & Sightseeing:

  • Visit Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque
  • Explore Topkapi Palace and Basilica Cistern
  • Walk through Sultanahmet Square and take photos

Facilities Included:

  • Entrance tickets to monuments
  • Guided historical tour
  • Comfortable coach transportation

Meals:

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Lunch at local restaurant (Turkish specialties)
  • Dinner at hotel or optional night tour meal
Day 3: Grand Bazaar & Spice Market

Activities & Sightseeing:

  • Visit Grand Bazaar for shopping souvenirs, carpets, jewelry
  • Explore Spice Bazaar and taste Turkish delights
  • Optional Bosphorus ferry cruise in evening

Facilities Included:

  • Guided shopping tour
  • Ferry tickets for Bosphorus cruise (if chosen)

Meals:

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Lunch in traditional Turkish cafe
  • Dinner at hotel
Day 4: Asian Side & Local Culture

Activities & Sightseeing:

  • Visit Kadikoy Market and Bagdat Street
  • Explore Çamlıca Hill for panoramic city views
  • Experience Turkish tea & coffee at local cafes

Facilities Included:

Meals:
